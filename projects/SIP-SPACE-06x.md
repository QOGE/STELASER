## 1. Executive Summary

The **HRX Glider Family** consists of purpose-built retrograde cargo vehicles optimized for the steep, reduced-velocity reentry profiles enabled by the Orbital Synchro-Rail.

These vehicles feature **wide lifting body geometry** to improve hypersonic lift, reduce peak g-loads, and increase survivability compared to standard Starship shapes.

## 2. Design Philosophy

- Optimized for **steep reentry** (-24° to -36° initial angle) at 4.0 – 5.13 km/s
- High hypersonic L/D (target 0.8 – 1.3)
- Flat-bottom compression lift surface
- Internal carbon fiber reinforcement frame
- Maximum cargo volume efficiency
- Compatibility with Synchro-Rail asymmetric operations

## 3. HRX Cargo Glider (Heavy Variant)

**Role**: Primary high-capacity retrograde cargo vehicle.

**Key Specifications:**
- Payload capacity: **80 – 120+ tonnes**
- Wide triangular / blended lifting body geometry
- Internal carbon fiber structural frame
- Designed for maximum downmass per flight
- Suitable for heavy lunar regolith, manufactured goods, and large equipment returns

## 4. HRX Nano Glider (Distributed Variant)

**Role**: Small, distributed cargo delivery. Multiple Nano Gliders can be launched per Synchro-Rail mission to split risk and optimize reentry performance.

**Key Specifications:**
- Payload capacity: **1 – 8 tonnes** per vehicle
- Smaller, lighter airframe → potentially better aerodynamic control and lower peak loads
- Higher number of vehicles per de-orbit window
- Easier to manufacture in volume
- Ideal for high-value or time-sensitive cargo distribution

**Advantages of Nano Approach:**
- Reduced risk per individual vehicle
- Better reentry performance due to lower ballistic coefficient
- Enables parallel testing and rapid iteration
- More flexible scheduling

## 5. Common Technical Features

- **Aerodynamic Shape**: Wide flat-bottom lifting body with blended edges
- **Structural Reinforcement**: Internal carbon fiber frame (protected by stainless steel skin)
- **Thermal Protection**: Optimized for short, high-angle reentry pulses
- **Operations**: Fully compatible with Synchro-Rail asymmetric delta-v and water ballast balancing

## 6. Strategic Value

The HRX Glider Family allows the Synchro-Rail to achieve its full potential by providing dedicated, high-performance retrograde cargo vehicles that are better suited to steep reentry conditions than standard Starship geometry.

This family enables both **high-volume bulk returns** (Cargo Glider) and **distributed, resilient delivery** (Nano Glider).
